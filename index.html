CFM is a student club located at the National Graduate School of Management in Kolea, Tipaza, Algeria. We focus on developing leadership and management skills for the future leaders of tomorrow. Our club offers workshops, networking events, and innovative projects to help members enhance their skills and prepare for successful careers. We are passionate about inspiring creativity and fostering collaboration among students.
